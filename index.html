AMY HWANG is a web developer and a design enthusiast who enjoys the challenge of learning new skills and creating visually engaging websites. Amy is well-versed in HTML, CSS, SCSS, JavaScript, Wordpress and PHP. She studied coding and design at Epicodus and worked at web agencies such as Ichor Studios and Department.nyc.
Amy describes herself as creative, compassionate, and detail oriented. When she's not coding, she can be found sipping tea, doodling or playing with her pets Maple and Sam.

MAPLE THE CAT is Amy's creative consultant of all the projects. She may not be tech-savvy but she knows what looks good.
The feline loves waking hoomins up early in the morning, chatting, cuddling and sitting on black clothes.
