Regiester2Vote

Register2Vote.org

Register2Vote was created for votes in states like Texas that don't allow online registration. When the form on the site is filled out, R2V send filled out register form with an envelope and a stamp.
related article: Texas best in country for new voter turnout, could affect tight races

Responsive Web Development | Nonprofit | HTML5 | SCSS | Custom Icons

When the organization reached out to me, the site had all the function ready to go, but had bare minimum style using BootStrap. In order to advoate the site, it needed clean styling that is apply friendly to voters of any age and race. In oreder to achieve the goal, after communicating with R2V, I set a typography, created custom icons and designed the main page and about page.

DOGPAW

Agency: Ichor Studios

DOGPAW is a non-profit that provides and maintains off-leash dog parks in SW Washington.

Sketch | Responsive Web Development | PHP | Wordpress | Nonprofit | Custom Icons | Maintanance

DOGPAW needed a new site that will tell the story of the organization and attract new members. In order to do that, we created a custom WordPress theme, reorganized the nav bar, created pages like registration, events, parks, member dashboard, and focused on call-to-action buttons such as member registeration and donation. During the process, we actively communicated with the in-house desinger and board members.

UpCart

Agency: Martini Media Group

Upcart is an e-commerce site that sells various types of carts and accessories.

Sketch | Responsive Web Development | Liquid | Shopify | E-Commerce | Custom Icons

Martini Media Group initially reached out to me for responsive design mockup of UpCart. The desktop version was already created, but they still needed mockups for smaller devices. Referring to the desktop mockup and the live site at the time, I mocked up the site for smaller devices using Sketch. Once the mockup was approved, I was asked to develop the site on Shopify with two other developers. I was in charge of footer and pages like Shop Upcart, Compare, Tips, and Tip. We had meetings using Zoom and communicated through Slack.
